Frequently Asked Questions

What types of roofing materials do you work with?
We specialize in metal roofing systems including standing seam, corrugated metal panels, and metal shingles. We also work with traditional materials like asphalt shingles, tiles, and commercial roofing systems. Our team can help you choose the best material for your specific needs and budget.
Do you offer free estimates?
Yes, we provide free, no-obligation estimates for all our roofing services. Our team will visit your site, assess your needs, and provide a detailed quote outlining the scope of work, materials, timeline, and costs.
How long does a typical roofing project take?
Project timelines vary depending on the size and complexity. Residential projects typically take 2-5 days, while commercial projects can range from 1-4 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the estimation process and work efficiently to minimize disruption.
What is on-site rollforming and what are its benefits?
On-site rollforming allows us to manufacture custom-length metal panels directly at your job site. Benefits include reduced material waste, perfect panel lengths, fewer seams, faster installation, and minimized handling damage. This is especially valuable for large commercial projects and custom residential work.
Do you provide warranties for your work?
Yes, we offer comprehensive warranties that cover both materials and workmanship. Our workmanship warranty typically ranges from 5-10 years, while material warranties vary by manufacturer and can extend up to 30 years or more for premium metal roofing systems.
Are you licensed and insured?
Absolutely. ALG Enterprises is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage to protect both our team and your property throughout the duration of every project.
What areas do you serve?
We serve commercial, residential, and institutional clients throughout the region. While we're based locally, our mobile rollforming capabilities allow us to efficiently serve projects across a wider geographic area. Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific location.
Can you work around my schedule?
Yes, we understand that roofing projects can be disruptive. We offer flexible scheduling options and will work with you to find the most convenient time for your project. We also take steps to minimize disruption and maintain clean, safe worksites.
